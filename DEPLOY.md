## To deploy on Vercel (Recommended):
1. Sign up for a free [Vercel](https://vercel.com) account
2. Click "New Project" and connect to your GitHub repository
3. Select the repo and use these settings:
   - Framework: Other
   - Root Directory: `client`
   - Build Command: (leave empty)
   - Output Directory: (leave empty)
4. Click "Deploy"
5. Share the URL with your special someone!

## To deploy on Netlify (Alternative):
1. Sign up for a free [Netlify](https://netlify.com) account
2. Drag and drop your entire `client` folder to their upload area
3. Share the provided URL

## To deploy anywhere else:
Just upload the contents of the `client` folder to any static web hosting.
